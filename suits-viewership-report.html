Viewership Trends

The show Suits started with a strong average viewership of 3.99 million in Season 1.
However, there was a noticeable decline over the years. Between Season 3 (3.54M) and Season 5 (2.13M), the average viewership decreased by 1.41 million viewers.

By the final season (Season 9), the shows average audience had dropped to just 0.90 million, reflecting a total drop of 3.09 million from its peak in Season 1.
